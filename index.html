What we do here:

As a movie club, we are a community of film enthusiasts who share a passion for the art of cinema. We come together regularly to watch movies and engage in lively discussions about them. Our club is a place where we can express our thoughts and opinions about films, share our knowledge and learn from one another. We select a diverse range of films to watch, from classic masterpieces to independent films and everything in between. We explore different genres, directors, and eras of cinema to broaden our horizons and deepen our appreciation of film. Our movie club is more than just a social gathering; it is a platform for intellectual exchange and artistic exploration. Through our club, we have formed lasting friendships and developed a deeper understanding of the power of cinema.
